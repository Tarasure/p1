About Phishing Website Detector

Phishing Website Detector is a deep learning-based project that aims to detect phishing websites with high accuracy. Our goal is to provide a reliable and efficient solution to help users avoid falling victim to phishing attacks.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safe and secure online environment by detecting and preventing phishing attacks. We believe that everyone has the right to browse the internet without fear of being scammed or compromised.
