About Cyberbullying Detector

Cyberbullying Detector is a machine learning-based project that aims to detect cyberbullying with high accuracy. Our goal is to provide a reliable and efficient solution to help users avoid falling victim to any kinds of bullying online.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safe and secure online environment by detecting and preventing cyberbullying. We believe that everyone has the right to browse the internet without fear of being bullied.
